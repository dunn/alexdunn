I’m a graduate student in Philosophy at UCSB

I study language and communication, and I’m particularly interested in proper names, semantic minimalism, and implicature. I wrote my undergraduate thesis on the metaphysics of things and their parts, arguing that even if ordinary things like tables and chairs exist, they are very different than we might at first think.

Projects

Harry Potter and philosophy

I wasn’t invited to contribute to the “Ultimate” Harry Potter and Philosophy book, but I’ve written a few introductions to philosophical ideas (time travel, philosophy of names) using Harry Potter as a jumping-off point. Comments are always appreciated!

Exists.js

Exists.js is a silly tool to generate symbolic representations of existence statements, such as “at least five things exist”, “exactly two things exist”, and “at most nine things exist”.

If you’re a scumbag analytic philosopher like me then this might actually come in handy, like when your so-called “friend” suggests that chairs are ontologically innocent.

Modern Philosophy in plain text

Modern Philosophy is a free textbook created by Walter Ott. It combines public-domain primary sources with supplementary material and study questions. Everything not in the public domain is licensed under CC BY-NC-SA 3.0.

This modified version of the text is written in Markdown. Markdown is a plain-text syntax that allows for easy conversion to other file formats. Using pandoc, Modern Philosophy can be easily converted to HTML, PDF, EPUB, and many other formats.
